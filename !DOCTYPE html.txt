              <p>Devised a comprehensive risk model using XGBoost that goes beyond traditional financial metrics. The need for a forward-looking, multi-modal predictor is crucial for modern investment and lending decisions. This project addresses that by fusing disparate data types for a high-accuracy, comprehensive risk score.</p>
